CATEGORIES

Exploring Material, Texture & Monumentality

Five material halls tracing the studio practice through touch, relief, casting, stone, and metal.

01 Process Hall Clay Sculptures Hand-shaped studies where gesture, anatomy, and raw surface become the first language of form. 02 Relief Hall Mural Design Layered wall narratives composed through relief, ornament, texture, and monumental storytelling. 03 Metal Hall Metal Works Forged and assembled pieces with reflective edges, patina, and tactile structural rhythm. 04 Stone Hall Stone Sculptures Permanent figures and architectural forms shaped through weight, shadow, and carved stillness. 05 Casting Hall Casting Works Molten material translated into enduring sculptural presence, texture, and archival permanence.
