ABOUT THE ARTIST

Transforming Stone, Metal & Form Into Timeless Narratives

Led by sculptor Sandeep Kumar Badatya, the studio brings together years of experience across stone sculpture, mural design, clay modeling, casting, and large-scale metal installations. Each work is approached as a dialogue between architecture, texture, and emotion — shaped through patience, craftsmanship, and material understanding.

From intricate wall murals to monumental sculptural forms, the practice is rooted in handcrafted detailing and timeless artistic presence. Every piece is designed not only to occupy space, but to transform it.
